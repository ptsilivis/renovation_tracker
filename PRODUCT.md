# Product context — Kampos Hub

register: product

## Users

Four adult family members (siblings/parents, not technical) co-managing the
renovation of an old stone summer house in Messinian Mani. They open the app to
answer concrete questions: how much is left in the budget, what is paid, which
task is next, what did we decide about the kitchen tiles. Mixed devices, mostly
phones and laptops, in daylight, unhurried. Greek is the primary language.

## Product purpose

One shared, trustworthy source of truth for renovation money and activities,
replacing scattered chats and spreadsheets. Money accuracy and legibility matter
most: numbers must read cleanly and never feel like a flashy fintech pitch.

## Brand & tone

Warm, calm, grounded in place. Aegean coastal palette: deep sea-teal with a
terracotta accent on soft tinted-stone neutrals. Literata (a warm serif) for
headings gives a human, letter-from-home feel; Noto Sans keeps data neutral and
clear. The feeling is a well-kept household ledger, not a dashboard demo.

## Anti-references

- Not a neon/gradient SaaS dashboard. No hero-metric template, no gradient text.
- Not cold corporate finance (navy + gold, dense fintech).
- Not playful/gamified. Restraint over decoration; delight lives in small,
  earned moments (a satisfying press, a smooth tab change), never on every pixel.

## Strategic principles

- Familiarity is a feature: standard top-nav + editable tables behave as expected.
- Every interactive control has default / hover / focus / active / disabled states.
- Motion conveys state only (feedback, reveal, tab change), 150–250 ms, ease-out.
- Legible money: tabular numerals, variance colored (over = terracotta, under = green).
- Bilingual EL/EN chrome; data stays in its source language.
